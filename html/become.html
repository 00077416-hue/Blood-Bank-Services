How to Become a Donor

  1. Check eligibility: Review our eligibility criteria on the Donation Info page.
  2. Register: Complete the registration form.
  3. Book an appointment: You can book through our contact number or visit a local donor centre.
  4. Donate: Follow pre-donation instructions (eat well, stay hydrated).
