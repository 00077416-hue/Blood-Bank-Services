Welcome to the officail website of Blood Bank Services (BBS)

Every day, lives are saved because someone chose to donate blood. At Blood Bank Services, we are committed to providing safe and reliable blood supplies to hospitals and patients in need across Trinidad and Tobago. Whether you are a first time donor or a regular volunteer, your contribution makes a real difference!!!

Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure a safe, adequate, and reliable blood supply for all patients in need. We are committed to promoting voluntary blood donation, maintaining the highest safety standards, and educating the public on the importance of blood donation in saving lives.

Why Donate Blood?

  • One donation can save up to three lives
  • Blood is needed daily for surgeries and emergencies
  • Donated blood cannot be manufactured
  • Regular donors help maintain a stable blood supply

Your Donation Matters

Blood cannot be stored forever, and regular donations are essential to ensure patients receive the care they need. Your donation today could save a life tomorrow.

Blood Shortage Alert !!!

Trinidad and Tobago faces a chronic blood shortage, consistently falling short of World Health Organization (WHO) recommendations due to insufficient voluntary donations.
