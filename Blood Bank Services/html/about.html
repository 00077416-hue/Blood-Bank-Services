About Blood Bank Services

Blood Bank Services (BSS) is committed to ensuring a safe, reliable supply of blood and blood products for hospitals and clinics in Trinidad abd Tobago nationwide. We operate donor centres, mobile donation units, and educational outreach programs.

Our services

  • Donor registration and booking
  • Mobile blood drives
  • Blood typing and infectious disease screening
  • Processing of blood components (red cells, plasma, platelets)
  • Distribution to healthcare facilities

How you can help?

Donate regularly, volunteer at drives, or host a mobile blood drive at your organisation.

Quality & Safety

Every donation follows strict testing and storage protocols that meet international standards. Donated blood is screened and labelled before release for transfusion to ensure patient safety.
